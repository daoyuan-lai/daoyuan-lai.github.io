About me

Daoyuan Lai is a third-year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Statistics and Actuarial Science, The University of Hong Kong. He is fortunate enough to be advised by Dr. Yan Dora Zhang and Prof. Pak-Chung Sham. Daoyuan enjoys cross-disciplinary research and is interested in statistical genetics, high-dimensional statistics, bioinformatics, and epidemiology. His current research focuses on data integration. That is, making better predictions by utilizing information from multiple datasets.
