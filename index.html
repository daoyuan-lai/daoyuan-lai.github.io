About me

I am a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Statistics and Actuarial Science at The University of Hong Kong. I have the good fortune of being advised by Dr. Yan Dora Zhang and Prof. Pak-Chung Sham. Additionally, I am collaborating with Dr. Tian Gu at Columbia University and Dr. Oscar Hernan Madrid Padilla at University of California, Los Angeles. I am passionate about cross-disciplinary research, particularly in statistical genetics, high-dimensional statistics, bioinformatics, and epidemiology. My research focuses on data integration, aiming to enhance predictions by leveraging information from multiple datasets. I am seeking job opportunities in both industry and academia.
